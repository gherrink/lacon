Make `collapse_repeated` fidelity-safe (REQ-engine-streaming-primitives, success-criterion #1). This is the engine-layer fix only. The git-status rule re-audit, fixtures, and spec update live in Plan 03 (Wave 2), which depends on the marker format this plan establishes.

Purpose: A dropped line must leave a clearly-marked lacon elision line, never a substituted/fabricated one (D-07, D-09).

</objective>
